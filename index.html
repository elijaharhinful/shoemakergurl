Hello, my name is Edna Frimpong.

I am the Shoemakergurl

Edna Frimpong, is an award winning entrepreneur popularly known as “the shoemakergurl“, thriving in a male dominated shoemaking industry. She authored the motivational book “ The Shoemakergul“.

Edna is the Co-Founder of Ahofade, a popular classic fashion brand in West Africa.Having a BA in Communication with a focus on Public Relations. She has made notable impact as a voice for females in the climate change and entrepreneurial space.

If she is not making shoes or called on stage for an award, she loves to share her experiences to help build the young generation and unleash their potentials through mentorship and coaching.
